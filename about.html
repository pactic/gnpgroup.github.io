FOUNDER & CHAIRMAN

GNP GROUP founded in 2008 is an Indian multi-business conglomerate headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra with businesses in Constructions, Consulting and Trading. GNP GROUP has a built a team which is a perfect blend of business acumen, gusto, knowledge and experience with unparalleled expertise in our industry. Our leadership works together to ensure customer satisfaction by combining premium quality office building with experienced management and leasing staff. Ours is a value based company driven by speed, quality and transparency. We are committed to helping the clients envision and materialize their business ventures and initiatives.

 
Mr. Girish Narayan Pawar is son of Mr. Narayan Pawar ( Founder & Chairman). His vision, dynamism and astute leadership have led the GNP GROUP to become one of today's leading business conglomerates.
